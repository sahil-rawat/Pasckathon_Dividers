Unique Features

The main features of our app that are for the safety of society in this uncertain situation.

Alloted 3 Days

As we know aadhar card is compulsory in our country hence every person has a unique aadhar id we randomly allocate 3 days to every aadhar card holder, That person is allowed to use public transport on alloted days only, This is to ensure social distancing in public transportations.

QR Code based Verification

You can travel in public transport hassel free by veryfying the qr code generated on the app, This is to ensure that only people allowed on particular day are travelling and each public transport is allowed to take limited people on board to maintain social distancing.

Charging Fine

A penalty is charged for not following and moving out on a non alloted day. The QR code verification Charges a penalty if a user not alloted on the day boards a public transport to discourage the people not following rules imoplied for social distancing.This Fine then can be used to improve our economy
